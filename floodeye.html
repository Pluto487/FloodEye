SDG 11 — Sustainable Cities & Communities

FloodEye strengthens urban disaster preparedness and community resilience. By integrating IoT sensor networks with citizen reporting and authority dashboards, we enable cities to detect, respond to, and recover from flood events faster — reducing displacement, infrastructure damage, and loss of life.
